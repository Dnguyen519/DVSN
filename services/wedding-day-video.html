FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How far in advance should we book? +
We recommend booking 8-12 months in advance, especially for peak season (May-October). However, we sometimes have last-minute availability, so it's always worth reaching out.
Do you travel for weddings? +
Absolutely! We love destination weddings and have filmed all across Canada and internationally. Travel fees apply for weddings outside the Greater Toronto Area.
Can we choose our own music? +
Yes! We encourage couples to share songs that are meaningful to them. We use licensed music to ensure your videos can be shared online without copyright issues.
When will we receive our wedding film? +
Typical delivery is 8-12 weeks for full films. Trailers are delivered within 2-3 weeks. Rush delivery options are available if you need your film sooner.
Do you also offer photography? +
While we specialize in videography, we work with talented photographers and can recommend trusted professionals or offer package deals with our partner photographers.
