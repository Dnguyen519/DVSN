FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does wedding videography cost in Toronto? +
Our Toronto wedding videography packages range from $1,500 to $5,500, depending on coverage hours, number of videographers, and deliverables. Most couples invest between $2,500-$3,500 for our popular Signature Collection package, which includes 8 hours of coverage and a 7-10 minute highlight film.
What's included in your wedding videography packages? +
All our wedding video packages include: professional 4K filming, multiple camera angles, drone footage (where permitted), professional audio recording of vows and speeches, cinematic color grading, licensed music, and digital delivery. Higher packages add raw footage, additional edits, and same-day edits.
How long should wedding videography coverage be? +
Most Toronto couples choose 8-10 hours of wedding videography coverage, which captures from getting ready through to the reception dancing. For intimate weddings, 6 hours may suffice. For larger celebrations or multi-day events, 12+ hours ensures no special moments are missed. We'll help determine the ideal coverage for your timeline.
What's the difference between a wedding film and raw footage? +
Your wedding film is a professionally edited, cinematic story of your day set to music - typically 7-15 minutes. Raw footage includes all unedited video clips captured throughout your wedding day (usually 3-6 hours of content). While the film captures the emotions and highlights, raw footage preserves complete ceremonies, speeches, and candid moments.
Do you offer wedding videography for small/intimate weddings? +
Yes! We love filming intimate weddings and elopements across Toronto and the GTA. We offer special micro-wedding packages starting at $1,500 for ceremonies under 50 guests. These smaller celebrations often allow for more creative, personalized filming approaches and result in incredibly emotional films.
How do you capture audio during the wedding ceremony? +
Crystal-clear audio is crucial for wedding films. We use professional wireless microphones on the officiant and groom, plus backup recorders near the couple. For speeches, we connect directly to the DJ's soundboard when possible. This multi-source approach ensures your vows and speeches are perfectly captured, even in challenging venues.
Can we see full wedding films you've created? +
Absolutely! While we showcase highlight reels publicly, we're happy to share 2-3 complete wedding films during your consultation. This gives you a better sense of our storytelling style, pacing, and how we structure a full wedding narrative. We'll match examples to your wedding style and venue type.
Do you use drones for wedding videography? +
Yes, we're licensed drone operators and include aerial footage when venue permits allow. Drone shots add cinematic scope to your wedding film, especially for outdoor ceremonies, waterfront venues, and grand entrances. We handle all permits and follow Transport Canada regulations for safe, legal operation.
