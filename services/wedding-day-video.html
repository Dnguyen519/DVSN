FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long will our wedding film be? +
The length depends on your package. Our highlight films range from 3-10 minutes, while documentary edits can be 30-60+ minutes. We focus on quality storytelling rather than just duration, ensuring every moment serves your story.
Do you use drones? +
Yes! Drone footage is included in our Signature and Luxury packages. We're licensed drone operators and will capture stunning aerial shots of your venue and celebration, weather and venue restrictions permitting.
How do you work with photographers? +
We work seamlessly with photographers, often sharing key moments and positions. We're experienced in coordinating shots without interfering with each other's work. We'll connect with your photographer before the wedding to ensure smooth collaboration.
Can we choose our own music? +
Absolutely! We can use your song choices where possible, or we'll select from our library of licensed music that matches your style. We ensure all music is properly licensed for online sharing.
What's your turnaround time? +
Delivery times vary by package: Essential (8 weeks), Signature (10 weeks), Luxury (6 weeks priority). We also offer rush delivery for an additional fee if you need your film sooner.
