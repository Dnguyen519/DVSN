ENGAGEMENT SESSION FAQ

How long is a typical engagement video session? +
Our engagement video sessions typically last 1-2 hours, depending on the package you choose. The Essential package includes a 1-hour shoot at one location, while our Signature package offers 2 hours at multiple locations. This gives us plenty of time to capture authentic moments without feeling rushed.
What's the difference between engagement videography and photography? +
While photography captures beautiful still moments, videography brings your love story to life with movement, sound, and emotion. You'll hear each other's laughter, see genuine reactions, and relive the energy of your relationship. Many couples choose both, but video offers a uniquely immersive way to remember this special time.
When should we book our engagement video session? +
We recommend booking your engagement session 3-6 months before your wedding. This timing allows you to use the video for save-the-dates and your wedding website. Spring and fall are particularly popular in Toronto for their beautiful colors and comfortable weather. Book early to secure your preferred date and location.
How do you make couples feel comfortable on camera? +
We understand being on camera can feel awkward at first! Our approach is conversational and relaxed. We'll guide you through natural movements and prompts that feel like a date rather than a video shoot. Most couples tell us they forget about the camera within the first 10 minutes. We focus on capturing your genuine connection, not stiff poses.
What locations work best for engagement videos in Toronto? +
Toronto offers incredible variety! Popular spots include the Distillery District for its historic charm, Toronto Islands for beach and skyline views, High Park for nature settings, and the Harbourfront for waterfront scenes. We also love discovering meaningful locations personal to your story - where you first met, had your first date, or got engaged.
Can we include our pet in the engagement video? +
Absolutely! Pets are family, and we love including them in your engagement film. Dogs are the most common, but we've filmed with cats, birds, and even horses. Just let us know in advance so we can plan for the best locations and ensure your pet feels comfortable too.
