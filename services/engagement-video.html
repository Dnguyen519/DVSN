ENGAGEMENT SESSION FAQ

What should we wear? +
We recommend coordinating (not matching) outfits in neutral or earthy tones. Avoid busy patterns and logos. Bring a second outfit for variety! We'll send a detailed style guide once you book.
What happens if it rains? +
Light rain can actually create beautiful, romantic footage! For heavy rain, we'll reschedule at no extra charge. We monitor weather closely and will communicate options 24 hours before your session.
Can we use the video for our wedding? +
Absolutely! Your engagement film is perfect for save-the-dates, wedding websites, or reception displays. We'll provide formats optimized for different uses.
Do you travel outside Toronto? +
Yes! We love exploring new locations. Travel within the GTA is included. For destinations beyond, we'll provide a custom quote based on location.
