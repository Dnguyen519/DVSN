TORONTO WEDDING VIDEOGRAPHY FAQ

How much does wedding videography cost in Toronto? +
Our Toronto wedding videography packages start at $2,500 for essential coverage and range up to $5,500+ for luxury packages. Most couples invest between $3,000-$4,000 for comprehensive coverage of their special day.
Do you travel outside Toronto? +
Yes! We cover all of the GTA including Mississauga, Vaughan, Richmond Hill, Markham, and beyond. We also travel to popular wedding destinations like Muskoka, Niagara, and Prince Edward County.
How far in advance should we book? +
Toronto's peak wedding season (May-October) books quickly. We recommend booking 8-12 months in advance. For popular dates and venues, even earlier is better. We're currently booking 2025 and 2026 weddings.
What's included in your packages? +
All packages include professional editing, color grading, licensed music, and online delivery. Depending on your package, you'll receive highlight films, documentary edits, drone footage, and more. We customize packages to match your needs.
