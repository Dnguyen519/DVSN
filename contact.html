Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance for weddings, and 4-6 weeks for corporate projects. However, we can sometimes accommodate rush projects depending on availability.

Do you travel outside of Toronto?

Yes! We serve all of the GTA and Southern Ontario. We're also available for destination weddings and corporate projects across Canada and internationally.

What's included in your packages?

All packages include professional 4K filming, color grading, sound design, and digital delivery. Specific inclusions vary by package - contact us for detailed information.

How long does post-production take?

Wedding films are typically delivered within 8-12 weeks. Corporate projects have a 2-4 week turnaround. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.
